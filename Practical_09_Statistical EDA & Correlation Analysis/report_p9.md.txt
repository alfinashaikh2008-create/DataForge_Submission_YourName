# Practical 9: Statistical EDA & Correlation Analysis

## Aim
To identify statistical relationships between numerical variables using correlation analysis.

## Procedure
1. Loaded the dataset into a Pandas DataFrame.
2. Filtered only numerical columns.
3. Generated the correlation matrix using `df.corr()`.
4. Identified the pair of variables with the highest positive correlation.
5. Calculated the skewness of the Sales column using `scipy.stats.skew`.
6. Exported the correlation matrix to a CSV file.

## Concept
Correlation measures the strength and direction of relationships between variables. Skewness indicates whether the distribution of a variable is symmetric or biased toward one side.

## Output Files
- 09_statistics.ipynb
- correlation_matrix.csv
- report_p9.md

## Conclusion
The correlation matrix was successfully generated, the strongest positive relationship between variables was identified, and the skewness of the Sales variable was analyzed.
